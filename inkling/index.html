How It Works

A safe, voice-driven tool for looking things up, learning, and exploring. Designed for kids and adults who benefit from hands-free, accessible interaction.

🎙️

Voice-First

Talk to Inkling like you'd talk to a person. No keyboard. No menus. Just speak.

🔒

Completely Private

No servers. No accounts. No analytics. All processing happens on-device with Apple Intelligence. Your conversations never leave your hands.

📡

Works Offline

No internet required. No WiFi. No cell data. Instant install, under 10 MB. Everything your device needs is already there.

Accessible First

VoiceOver, Voice Control, Dynamic Type, Reduced Motion. Built for people with autism, dyslexia, and motor disabilities. Everyone benefits from the same interface.

🧒

Safe for Kids

No ads. No in-app purchases. No social features. No links out. COPPA-compliant by architecture because there is no data to collect.

🧑

Useful for Adults

Not a toy. A genuinely useful hands-free tool for anyone who benefits from voice-first, accessible interaction.
