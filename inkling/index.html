How It Works

Designed for anyone who wants an assistant that's simpler, calmer, and more accessible than most.

📡

Works Offline

Under 10 MB. Opens instantly from an App Clip, with no install prompt, no Side Button, no waiting. Everything's on the device and curated, not streamed from the open web.*

🎙️

Voice-First

Talk to Otto like you'd talk to a person. No keyboard. No menus. Just speak.

🔒

Completely Private

No servers. No accounts. No analytics. All processing happens on-device with Apple Intelligence. Your conversations never leave your hands.

🧒

Safe for Kids

No ads. No in-app purchases. No social features. No links out. COPPA-compliant by architecture because there is no data to collect.

🧑

Loved by Adults

Feels like a toy. Works like a tool. Made to be fun for everyone.

* Otto runs on Apple Intelligence. Weather comes from Apple WeatherKit. Ottotune requires an Apple Music subscription. None of these route through a web browser.

Built on Principles

No notifications begging for attention. No streaks. No dark patterns. Soft-spoken software that respects your time and your child's attention.
