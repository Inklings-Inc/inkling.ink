Ink Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 25, 2026

The short version

Ink does not collect personal data. The app has no accounts, no ads, no third-party analytics SDKs, no tracking SDKs, and no Inklings servers. Your coloring pages stay on your device and, when iCloud is on, sync through your own private iCloud account so they appear on your other devices. That storage is part of your personal Apple account, not an Inklings server. Inklings never receives or sees your pages.

What data does Ink collect?

None by Inklings.

Ink keeps your coloring pages and app activity on your device. When iCloud is on, your pages also sync through your own private iCloud account so they stay in step across your devices. This is your personal Apple storage, not an Inklings server, and Inklings cannot read it. We do not receive your prompts, drawings, generated pages, saved pages, device identifiers, location, contacts, or usage history.

Accounts and sign-in

Ink does not require or support user accounts. There is no sign-in, email collection, user registration, or profile system.

Image generation

Ink uses Apple Intelligence on your device to create coloring pages. Prompts and generated images are processed on your device. Ink does not send prompts or images to Inklings servers, and Inklings cannot view or store the images you create.

Data stored on your device

Ink may store coloring pages, drawings, prompt text, app settings, and recent app state locally on your device so the app can work normally. Deleting the app removes this data from that device. Any pages you have synced through your private iCloud stay in your own iCloud account until you delete them there. Device backups are handled by your iPhone or iPad settings.

Sharing and export

If you choose to share or export a page, Apple’s system share sheet and the destination app or service handle that action. Ink does not receive information about where you share a page.

Analytics, tracking, and advertising

Ink contains no advertising frameworks, no tracking SDKs, no third-party analytics SDKs, and no tracking pixels. Apple may provide standard, aggregated App Store and crash information to developers when users have enabled sharing diagnostics with app developers in their device settings. We do not use that information to identify users.

Children’s privacy

Ink is designed for family use, including use by children. Because Ink does not collect personal data, does not use behavioral advertising, and does not require accounts, there is no child profile or child personal information for Inklings to collect, sell, or share.

Third-party services

Ink does not include third-party SDKs. The app is built with Apple first-party frameworks.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update this page and the effective date above. Material privacy changes will be reflected before the updated app is submitted for review when practical.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at ottoreply@inkling.ink.

Ink is made by Inklings, LLC.
