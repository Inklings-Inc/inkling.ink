Three layers of learning

Not every word needs an emoji. The scaffold system matches the right tool to each word type.

🏠

Objects → Emoji

Concrete things get emoji. House, dog, sun, book, cat. Instant visual meaning that a toddler or language learner grasps immediately.

⬆️

Actions → Animation

Actions become motion. "Jumped" bounces. "Ran" slides. The child sees the action happen. No symbol needed. The movement IS the meaning.

···

Function words → Repetition

"The," "a," "in," "on." These words don't need scaffolding. They need exposure. Fifty sentences and "the" becomes a sight word.
