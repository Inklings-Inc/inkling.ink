What We Build

Private, accessible, on-device software. Safe enough for a two-year-old. Useful enough that you'll keep it for yourself.

🔒

Private by architecture

No servers. No accounts. No analytics. Everything runs on your device. Your data never leaves your hands.

Accessible first

Not a bolt-on. The core mechanic. Built for people with autism, dyslexia, and motor disabilities. Designed so everyone benefits from the same interface.

🧸

Montessori-aligned

Follow the learner's interest. One concept at a time. Concrete before abstract. These aren't prompt tricks. They're product rules.

🪵

Deliberately not noisy

No ads. No notifications begging for attention. No dark patterns. Soft-spoken software that respects your time.

📡

Works completely offline

No internet required. No WiFi. No cell data. Instant install, under 10 MB. Everything your device needs is already there.

🧪

Research-backed

Every design decision is grounded in cognitive science, developmental psychology, and accessibility research. Read the research →
